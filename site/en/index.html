Schedule Performers Team

“TILT is a fantastic festival! Excellent selection of teachers, a friendly community and a charming city make for a highly recommended improv weekend.”

Aree Witoelar (IDN/NOR)

“TILT is like Tallinn itself, small enough to make real connections, big enough to have a great time and full of wonderful people. A festival with a heart in a gorgeous city- what more could you ask for!”

Ella Galt (ESP)

“I love the atmosphere and the variety of performances. Everything is so cosy and well organised.”

Katarzyna Chmara (POL)

“The Tilt Festival is a high-intensity blast of fun - which, as we know, is fun - and creativity - which is also fun, as Tilt proves over and over again. Eighteen performances and eighteen workshops packed into three days; dozens of improvisers from all over the world; it's a blast. Oh, and Tallinn is wonderful too. Go.”

Phil Whelans (GBR)

“TILT is an awesome and intimate festival - You get an opportunity to be close to every participant! Go visit!”

Stefan Pagels Andersen (DNK)

“I have been to several festivals and Tilt is really worth coming and even coming back!”

Schedule

19 September

10:00 - 13:00 Workshop Phil Whelans - Fiendish Plotting
10:00 - 13:00 Workshop Stefan Pagels Andersen - How to make improv easy
14:00 - 17:00 Workshop Sophia Lierenfeld - Trust Your Sexuality
14:00 - 17:00 Workshop Dan Seyfried - Unplug Your Brain
 
18:10 - 19:15 Show “Bonnie & Clyde” & “The Dropthrough”
19:30 - 21:00 Show Rahel ja Mihkel (EST) - “Missed Calls” & Improteater IMPEERIUM (EST) – “On the writer’s exclusive texts”
21:15 - 22:45 Show Us boys who go out together (CZE): “The Bohemian Tragedy” & Stacey Smith (USA) - “Stacejam”

20 September

10:00 - 13:00 Workshop Phil Lunn - Discovering Songs
10:00 - 13:00 Workshop Ella Galt - Silence: Loud & Clear
14:00 - 17:00 Workshop Małgorzata Różalska - A Simple Lovestory
14:00 - 17:00 Workshop Stacey Smith - Jump on it
 
18:15 - 19:15 Show Mixer shows: “The Metronome” & “Cloud Atlas”
19:30 - 21:00 Show Jaa !mproteater ja Improkutsikad (EST) - “Maestro” & Ruutu10 (EST) - "That was a joke, right?!"
21:15 - 22:45 Show Dan & Ella (FRA/ESP) - “Origami Swan” & Doris Likes Everything (FIN) - "Loser Circus"

21 September

10:00 - 13:00 Workshop Aree Witoelar - Being a Dream Partner
10:00 - 13:00 Workshop Stefan Pagels Andersen - The ICC Theatre long-form structure “The Mono scene”
14:00 - 17:00 Workshop Václav Wortner - Be a Troublemaker, Fuel the Scene
14:00 - 17:00 Workshop Jeron Dewulf - Stand-up and Improv
 
18:15 - 19:15 Show “Sõnadeta: Heliline teos!” & “Portmanteau”
19:30 - 21:00 Show Troika (LVA/RUS) - “Journey” & ICC Theatre (DNK)
21:15 - 22:45 Show The Improv Speakeasy (NOR) - “Alice in Improvland” & 7 Women of Different Ages (POL)
